My favorite polynomial

I chose the polynomial 10x^9 - 8x^7 + 6x^5 + 4x^3 - 2x^1 because I just wanted something that was simple and kind of fun in a way. It is simple in the fact that it is in decending order when counting the coefficients and the exponents. It is fun in the sense that you can see the numbers being counted down and that the result is a huge number.
